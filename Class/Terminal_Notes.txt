Notes on Terminal Commands

pwd: shows you where you are (ex: monicagoebel, Desktop, MAT341_Self, etc.)
ls: lists every file/folder in your directory
cd: change directory (up 1 directory)
	cd Desktop/MAT341_Self/Class: brings you to Class
	cd ~: brings you all the way to monicagoebel
vi filename: opens up text box to type in
clear: clears your screen
history: shows everything you've typed in the past
